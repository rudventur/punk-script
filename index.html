PunkScript — The Language

A personally-adaptive symbolic programming language where punctuation IS syntax and the interpreter adapts to the user, not the other way around.

ConceptSummary
Punctuation as syntax. ends statements, ! executes, ? is conditional, ... loops
Emoticon operators:@D = success, >:@( = error, :/ = warning
Personal dictionaryMap your own words/symbols to operations
Spelling forgivenessFuzzy matching built in — dyslexia is a dialect, not a bug

FizzBuzz in PunkScript

count: zero.
keep going... [
  add one to (count).
  (count) beats one hundred? stop!
  fizz: (count) split by three is zero.
  buzz: (count) split by five is zero.
  (fizz) & (buzz)? say "FizzBuzz"!
  - (fizz)? say "Fizz"!
  - (buzz)? say "Buzz"!
  - say (count)!
]
